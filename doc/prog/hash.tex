possible keys. A set of hash functions
$H = {h : U → { 0 , 1 , 2 , . . . , m − 1 }}$



is called a universal family if for any two keys
$x, y ∈ U, x ̸ = y$ the probability of collision

$Pr [h(x) = h(y )] <= 1/m$
